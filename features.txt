Unique Features for Amazon Now – Reimagining Urgent Shopping
Situation-to-Cart
User describes the situation instead of searching products.
Example:
“Friends coming over” → chips, cold drinks, snacks
“Kid has fever” → medicine, ORS, thermometer
Photo-to-Cart
User uploads fridge/kitchen photo.
AI detects missing/low-stock items and creates a refill cart.
Use Meta.
WhatsApp-style Shopping
Chat-based shopping instead of traditional browsing.
Example:
“Need maggi ingredients” → Maggi, onion, tomato, masala
Urgency Mode
AI classifies urgency:
High → medicines, baby products
Medium → groceries
Low → snacks
Prioritizes fastest delivery.
Predict Before Need
Learns buying patterns.
Example:
“Milk usually finishes today” → one-tap reorder suggestion.
Smart Bundles
Suggests complementary products automatically.
Example:
Bread → butter, jam, eggs
